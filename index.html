Games Artists Play Spring 2017

Course Objectives

The main objective of this course is to have fun making art. And make art that is fun!

Work? Play? Art? Game?

p1: group performance

p2: utopian community

p3: player project

p4: fuck the system

p5: collection

Grading

Come to class! Participate! Do the readings. Complete assignments and projects as given, and your grade will take care of itself. Your grade will be based on the following:

35% attendance, class participation

15% midterm

25% final project

25% smaller assignments

Attendance and Participation at critiques is essential.
