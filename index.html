Course Objectives

The main point of this course is to have fun making art. And make art that is fun!

Work? Play? Art? Game?

Below are links to some ideas that may help you think about the five games that you should make in this class.

game 1: group performance

game 3: player project

game 4: fuck the system

game 5: collection

Grading

Come to class! Participate! Do the readings. Complete assignments and projects as given, and your grade will take care of itself. Your grade will be based on the following:

35% attendance, class participation

15% midterm

25% final project

25% smaller assignments

Attendance and Participation at critiques is essential.
